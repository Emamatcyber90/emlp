Exercise 2.4.1

What are the values of the following expressions?

(a) #2(3, 4, 5)
    4

(b) hd([3, 4, 5])
    3

(c) tl([3, 4, 5])
    [4, 5]

(d) explode("foo")
    [#"f", #"o", #"o"]

(e) implode([#"f", #"o", #"o"])
    "foo"

(f) "c"::["a", "t"]
    ["c", "a", "t"]

(g) ["c", "o"]@["b", "o", "l"]
    ["c", "o", "b", "o", "l"]

(h) concat(["c", "a", "t"])
    "cat"
