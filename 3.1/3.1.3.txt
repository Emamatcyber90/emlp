Exercise 3.1.3

Suppose we execute the following sequence of definitions:

    val a = 2;
    fun f(b) = a * b;
    val b = 3;
    fun g(a) = a + b;

Give the value of the following expressions:

(a) f(4)
    8

(b) f(4) + b
    11

(c) g(5)
    8

(d) g(5) + a
    10

(e) f(g(6))
    18

(f) g(f(7))
    17
